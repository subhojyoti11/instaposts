--- AI PARADIGM SHIFTS (201+) ---
201. AI Looping vs Prompting
- Focus: Why single-shot prompting is dead. How agentic loops work: the model receives a Goal, generates a response, critiques its own output, writes its next prompt, and iterates until the goal is met — acting as its own QA manager.
- Key angle: Prompting = command → answer (you do the heavy lifting). Looping = Goal → self-critique → self-prompt → repeat (the model does the heavy lifting). Real-world shift already happening in Cursor, Devin, and OpenAI's o3 reasoning loops.
